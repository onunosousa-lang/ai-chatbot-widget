Integration Code:

Try it out:

Click the chatbot button in the bottom-right corner and ask a question like:

  • "What are your pricing plans?"
  • "How does the chatbot work?"
  • "Tell me about your services"

Customization:

You can easily customize the chatbot by changing the data attributes:

  • data-primary-color - Change the theme color
  • data-company-name - Your company name
  • data-welcome-message - Custom greeting
  • data-position - Widget position (bottom-right, bottom-left, etc.)
  • data-logo - Your company logo URL
